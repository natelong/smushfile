What is Smushfile?

Smushfile is a service that allows you to smush a bunch of text files (like Javascript or CSS) together. Why would you want to do this? Because it's way faster than asking your users to download a bunch of separate files. You could totally do this yourself, but it's way easier to have an automated process take care of boring stuff like this.
